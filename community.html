Common Questions

FAQ

What exactly is the weekly call? +
Every week Chris hosts a live group video call, open to all members. It's an open Q&A format — members bring questions about their stores, get feedback, and discuss what's happening in ecommerce. Calls are also recorded, so if you can't make it live you can watch the replay at any time.
How does direct messaging with Chris work? +
As a community member, you have access to message Chris directly inside the platform. He responds to member questions personally — store feedback, strategy, tools, email, ads, whatever you're working through. This isn't a support inbox with a team — it's Chris directly.
What kinds of topics does the community cover? +
Anything relevant to running an ecommerce store. Paid advertising (Meta, Google, TikTok), email & retention, Klaviyo, Shopify, product and sourcing, operations and fulfilment, customer service, tools and tech, conversion rate optimization, and general business discussions. Members drive the conversations — it goes wherever the group needs it.
Do I need to be doing a certain revenue level to join? +
No minimum revenue required. The community has members at every stage — from people just launching their first store to established brands doing multiple 7 figures. The advice and discussions are useful regardless of where you are. That said, you'll get the most from it if you're actively working on a store.
How do I cancel if I decide it's not for me? +
Cancel anytime, directly from your account dashboard — no need to email or call anyone. Your membership stays active until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no exit interviews, no retention tactics. Just cancel and you're done.
Where does the community take place? +
After joining, you'll receive onboarding instructions with access to the private community platform. Weekly calls are hosted via video — you'll get calendar invites and a join link as part of your welcome.
Is this like a course or coaching program? +
Neither. It's a community — ongoing, live, and peer-driven. There's no fixed curriculum. It's built around the weekly call and real-time discussions with other operators. Think of it less like a course and more like being part of a professional peer group that happens to include someone very experienced running it.
